Admission Requirements and Procedures at IUO:

- Applicants must have a minimum of 5 credits in O'Level including English and Mathematics.
- Candidates must participate in the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) conducted by JAMB.
- Post-UTME screening is mandatory for all candidates.
- Direct Entry candidates must have a relevant diploma or A-level certificate.
- Application forms are available online via the IUO admission portal.
- Application deadlines are usually in the first quarter of the academic year.
