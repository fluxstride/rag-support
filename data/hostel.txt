Hostel Accommodation and Campus Facilities:

- IUO provides on-campus hostel accommodation for students.
- Hostels are segregated by gender and managed by hostel wardens.
- Accommodation application is part of the registration process and subject to availability.
- Facilities include study rooms, common areas, internet access, and 24/7 security.
- Hostels are regularly maintained and students are expected to adhere to hostel rules.
- Additional campus facilities include libraries, sports complexes, and health centers.
