Course Offerings and Registration:

- IUO offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses across various faculties.
- Registration for courses is done online through the IUO student portal.
- Students are required to register for courses before the deadline, typically two weeks after semester starts.
- Course units range from 2 to 4 credit units.
- Elective and compulsory courses must be completed as specified by the faculty.
- Students must consult their academic advisors before course registration to ensure proper course selection.
